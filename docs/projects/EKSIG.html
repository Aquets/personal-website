EKSIG

2022

Website for the conference Eksig 2023: From Abstractness to Concreteness. Experiential knowledge and the role of prototypes in design research, hosted by the Department of Design of Politecnico di Milano.

An interactive and generative cover has been designed for the website to symbolize the transition from the abstract nature of a concept to the tangible reality of a prototype.
